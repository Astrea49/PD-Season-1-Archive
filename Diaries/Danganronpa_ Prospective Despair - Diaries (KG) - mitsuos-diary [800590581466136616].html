DAY 01
. . Ah jeez... What the hell did I get myself into... My first journal entry and all I can muster are feelings of despair and hopelessness. Alright, focus Kataoka. To recall and get a better understanding of what's going on here, I've decided to log everything here by the time the day ends. What uses one or even I myself can make out of these is still cloudy, since I haven't done my portion of looking around yet. Anyways, here's my recount of what happened today; I open my eyes to the scene of an unfamiliar and futuristic room with lots of people just as clueless as I am regarding where we were. One thing I did feel immediately after would be this feeling of disconnect with my body and the floor beneath me, one that made me feel that I could fall at any given moment. It felt as if I was in a tall structure of some kind. I tried to pay attention to everyone's response, which were varied in a lot of different ways, particularly through their attitude, their general feel, so on and so forth. They were all very interesting characters, ones that particularly do not mingle with the crowd but are instead the center of attention. The ones leading crowds. I, for one, am more of the former. My presence was basically non-existant as these peoplr exerted their colorful personalities and backgrounds.

. . Though soon after, our 'host' made his presence known via the table in front of us projecting his moving image. The fellow struck me as cold the first time I saw him. He definitely feels like an antagonist and his words after were concise and short. What a thrill! Such a menacing figure enacting his clearly superior authority upon us strangers and immediately stating his objective with no more than just a few words~, truly a spectacle to beh-- Ahem. Anyways, it was around this moment that everyone got equally perplexed but with varied reactions as per usual. While most were assuming this was some elaborate joke of some kind, those words strike me as slightly terrifying. I know that he purposefully didn't disclose any further details to get some kind of reaction out everyone but his word choice was enough to make everyone ruffle their feathers. Of course, there were among us skeptics that did not believe his words and underestimated its weight.
. . . So. For good measure, I assume, he dropped a... ... A... mangled corpse thats been tampered with so much its beyond any form of recovery or identification. That awful sight is still fresh in my mind despite it being hours after witnessing it. How can one even forget about that scene... Regardless, this solidified his stance and silenced those who doubted him. Then, we all scattered to our own ways. I heard some were searching the area, some were probably like me. A crying mess thats unable to accept what sort of shitshow they got themselves into. I, paid no heed to everything else and just sobbed the day away. There's a few things I can take from this summary. For one thing, whoever the hell this man is, might not be alone. He might have backers or perhaps he, himself, is a pawn of something much bigger. I cant draw any conclusions until Im certain I have enough evidence. Next is that, Im practically invisible to everyone. Granted, we all just met today, so I cant expect them to know literally everyone on a first name basis. What I mean by that is that my presence is, well, pathetic. Compared to these people, Im not even close to their standing in society. Kinda makes me feel a bit shy. They're all equally amazing people and Im just...An average joe, at best. Well, we're all stuck in here together now, so we gotta stick together and help each other out, considering that's the best possible way we can all get out of here unscathed. So, I hope I can get along with them tommorrow. I may be invisible but I want to be around them too. I guess that concludes day one. I might as well head to bed after this and look around starting tomorrow.
